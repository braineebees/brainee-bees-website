About Our Play Area

Our vibrant and safe play environment is designed for children aged 2 to 5 to explore, play, and grow. With indoor and outdoor activity zones, your child develops social, motor, and creative skills in a joyful setting.

Activities We Offer

  • 📚 Story Time and Puppet Shows
  • 🧩 Puzzle Play and Sensory Games
  • 🌼 Nature Walks and Outdoor Games
  • 🎶 Music, Dance & Rhymes
  • 🧸 Free Play in Our Safe Toy Zone

Why Parents Love It

  • 🧠 Early learning foundations through play
