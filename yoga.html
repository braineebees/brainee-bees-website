Why Yoga for Kids?

Yoga helps children become more aware of their body, mind, and emotions. It improves flexibility, concentration, self-esteem, and emotional regulation — all while having fun.

What We Offer

  • 🧘 Story-based yoga flows
  • 🌬️ Breathing and mindfulness for calmness
  • 🧍‍♀️ Balance and posture activities
  • 💖 Emotion awareness and self-love practices
  • 🎵 Music + movement meditations

Age Group

Our yoga sessions are designed for kids aged 3–10 years. Each session is tailored to the child's age and energy level.

Session Schedule

  • 🧘 Weekend Special Story Yoga
